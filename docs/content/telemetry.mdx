## Metrics

Metrics describe rollout throughput, training step duration, reward distributions, and queue pressure.

## Tracing

Trace spans connect launcher actions, rollout execution, environment calls, and training updates.

meter.record("grl.rollouts.completed", completed_rollouts)

## Logs

Structured logs capture run identifiers and policy versions for later debugging.

## Dashboards

Grafana panels summarize cluster health and training progress during long-running jobs.
